Coffee Break Lovers

by Wahnzettel

We always wait
For that time of the day
Our coffee cups
We take out from display

Warm afternoon
Aroma fills the room
We're together
And simple smiles just bloom

You always like
Double-shot espresso
While I drnk based
On mood or scenario

We sip coffee
While indulging silence
We are lovers
Only in these moments
Fifteen minutes
We're back to our stations
Break time is done
So does our affection
